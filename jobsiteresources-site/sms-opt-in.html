Jobsite Resources SMS Opt-In

Public consent and messaging program information for Jobsite Resources Notifications.

Messaging Program

Jobsite Resources sends operational text messages for construction safety, onboarding, project document, certification, signature, and field communication workflows.

Messages are sent by Jobsite Resources or a customer company using Jobsite Resources. Recipients are employees, foremen, supervisors, office users, or project contacts who provide a mobile number and actively agree to receive operational messages.

How Users Opt In

Users may opt in when completing a company onboarding or intake request, when an authorized office user enters their mobile number for project notifications, or when a company collects written permission from a worker or project contact. The consent checkbox is not pre-checked.

Example Intake Request Consent

I agree to receive operational text messages from Jobsite Resources related to onboarding, safety documents, project notices, certification reminders, signature links, and account updates. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help and STOP to opt out. Consent is not a condition of purchase. See the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.

Message Examples

Example 1: Jobsite Resources: Your PAL onboarding packet is ready. Open your secure intake link to complete required forms before arriving onsite. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

Example 2: Jobsite Resources: A safety document is ready for your review and signature. Open the secure link from your supervisor. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

Example 3: Jobsite Resources: Certification reminder: one or more employee certifications are expired or expiring soon. Log in to review. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

Help and Opt-Out

Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to opt out of further text messages. After opting out, users may need to contact their employer or Jobsite Resources for non-SMS communication options.
