Jobsite Resources Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

Jobsite Resources provides digital safety, project, onboarding, document, and field communication tools for construction and environmental service teams.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information, employee and project records, safety documents, certification records, uploaded files, photos, signatures, email addresses, phone numbers, usage logs, and other information submitted through the service.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to operate the platform, manage safety and project records, support employee onboarding, send operational notices, provide customer support, maintain security, and preserve business records.

Text Message Communications

If a user provides a mobile number and agrees to receive messages, Jobsite Resources may send operational messages such as onboarding links, safety document links, project reminders, certification notices, document signature links, or account-related updates. Message frequency varies based on app use. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help, where supported.

No Sale or Marketing Sharing

Jobsite Resources does not sell personal information or share mobile phone numbers with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

Mobile opt-in data and text messaging consent are not shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Service Providers

We may use trusted service providers for hosting, storage, email delivery, text messaging, authentication, analytics, and support. These providers are used only to operate and secure the service.

Record Retention

Safety, project, employee, and compliance records may be retained for long-term business, legal, safety, and insurance needs unless an authorized administrator applies an approved retention policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to notifications@jobsiteresources.com.
