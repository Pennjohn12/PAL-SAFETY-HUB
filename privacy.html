Jobsite Resources Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

Jobsite Resources provides digital safety, project, onboarding, and field communication tools for construction and environmental service teams.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information, employee onboarding information, project records, safety documents, certification records, uploaded files, photos, signatures, email addresses, phone numbers, and app activity needed to provide the service.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the app, maintain safety and project records, send requested notifications, support employee onboarding, manage project documentation, provide administrative access, and improve reliability and security.

Text Message Communications

If a user provides a mobile number and agrees to receive messages, Jobsite Resources may send operational messages such as onboarding links, safety document links, project reminders, certification notices, or account-related updates. Message frequency varies based on app use. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help, where supported.

No Sale or Marketing Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share mobile opt-in data, consent, or phone numbers with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

Service Providers

We may use trusted service providers, such as hosting, storage, email, text messaging, and AI providers, only to operate the service and perform requested app functions. These providers are not authorized to use personal information for their own marketing.

Data Retention

Safety, project, employee, and compliance records may be retained for long-term business, legal, safety, and insurance needs unless an authorized administrator applies an approved retention policy.

Security

We use access controls, authentication, role-based permissions, logging, and secure cloud services to help protect records. No system is perfect, but we work to keep app data protected and available.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to notifications@jobsiteresources.com.
